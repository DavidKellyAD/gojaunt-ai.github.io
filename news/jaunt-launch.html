Revolutionizing Travel with an AI-Powered Personal Concierge

UNDER EMBARGO UNTIL

January 31, 2024

This New Year, Jaunt, the latest innovation in travel technology, invites you to make a resolution to become a traveler, not a tourist. As an AI-powered personal concierge, Jaunt is designed for both travelers and local explorers, offering a unique and interactive way to discover hidden gems in cities worldwide.

Jaunt revolutionizes exploration with its advanced AI technology, going beyond a standard travel app. It acts as a smart companion that understands your preferences, offering personalized recommendations. Whether planning a trip abroad or exploring your hometown, Jaunt’s proprietary AI tailors suggestions for destinations, activities, and experiences that match your interests.

The app’s engaging and interactive chat interface uses natural language to converse with Jaunt's AI, making travel planning seem effortless. Its intuitive design invites users to swipe right on appealing recommendations, creating a fun and interactive experience.

Jaunt’s mission lies in guiding users from being tourists to true travelers. The app focuses on uncovering hidden treasures and less-trodden paths, offering insights into unique and authentic experiences often overlooked by standard travel guides. It provides insights into restaurants, museums, activities, and more for a richer experience.

With its mobile-first approach, Jaunt ensures that a world of exploration is always at your fingertips, whether planning your next adventure at home or navigating a new city. Jaunt stands as your go-to guide for discovering the extraordinary. As we embark on a new year, Jaunt invites you to join a community of explorers who seek more than the ordinary. The app is available for download on iOS and Android devices. Join Jaunt to turn your travel dreams into reality. For more information, visit gojaunt.ai or download Jaunt from the app store.
