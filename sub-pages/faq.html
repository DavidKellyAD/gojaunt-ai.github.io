Frequently Asked Questions

Why is Jaunt not free to use?

If something is free, you are the product. Jaunt charges for its services to ensure that we can continuously provide you with an amazing user experience and continue to deliver new and exciting features without compromising our integrity and the privacy of our users.

How are your recommendations generated?

Our recommendations are powered by an engine that learns from your preferences and previous choices. Because of this, our platform understands the context and nuances of each ask, the circumstances around it, the individual preferences of the user, and the choices you have made previously. In the beginning, your recommendations may be broad and general, but the more you leverage the Jaunt platform, the more personalized and intimate your recommendations will become.

What type of travel experiences can Jaunt help me plan?

Jaunt can assist you in planning a wide array of travel experiences. Whether you’re looking for kid-friendly spots on a road trip, coffee shops, culturally rich museums, or restaurants catering to specific dietary needs, Jaunt has you covered.

How does Jaunt ensure the quality of its recommendations?

Jaunt’s engine leverages advanced AI technology and personalization modeling among other various methods to always ensure quality. Our engine analyzes a wide range of factors including user preferences, past choices, and current trends to provide tailored suggestions. Additionally, Jaunt incorporates user feedback and reviews into its recommendation system, continuously refining and improving the quality of its suggestions. This multi-faceted approach ensures that the recommendations are not only personalized but also of high quality, catering to the specific needs and interests of each user.

Is Jaunt suitable for planning both domestic and international trips?

Yes! Jaunt can be used to plan trips and outings both in the United States and abroad.

Can Jaunt accommodate specific preferences or needs, like family-friendly or adventure travel?

You bet we can! In fact, that’s our specialty. Jaunt is expertly designed to cater to a wide range of travel preferences and needs. Whether you’re seeking family-friendly destinations, adventure-filled excursions, or anything in between, Jaunt’s sophisticated recommendation engine adeptly personalizes recommendations to match your unique interests. By considering your specific requirements and preferences, Jaunt ensures that every travel suggestion aligns with what’s most important to you, making every trip memorable and tailored to your desires.

How does Jaunt handle personal data and privacy?

Jaunt is deeply committed to protecting user data and privacy. We take extensive measures to safeguard your information and ensure it’s used solely for enhancing our product. We firmly stand against selling any user data. Every piece of information collected by the Jaunt platform is utilized to improve our services, ensuring a better and more personalized user experience. Your privacy is our top priority, and we continuously update our practices to maintain the highest standards of data protection and confidentiality.

Are there any locations where Jaunt’s services are not available?

While Jaunt can answer questions about nearly any destination globally, at this time, our service is only available to customers based in the US.

How can I provide feedback or suggestions to improve Jaunt?

To share your feedback or suggestions for improving Jaunt, you have several convenient options. You can email us at feedback@gojaunt.ai for any detailed insights or comments. Alternatively, for quick and easy feedback, use the in-app feature – either respond directly to the Jaunt AI or navigate to your account settings to submit your thoughts. We value your input highly, as it helps us to continually enhance your Jaunt experience.

Can Jaunt help with last-minute travel plans?

Our platform is equipped to offer quick and relevant recommendations, no matter how soon your trip is. Whether you're planning a spontaneous getaway or need to make unexpected travel arrangements, Jaunt's AI swiftly provides you with personalized suggestions to suit your immediate needs, ensuring your travel is always stress-free and enjoyable.

How are you different from more traditional travel services or other AI powered platforms in the space?

Jaunt differentiates itself in several key ways from traditional services and other AI-powered platforms. Jaunt’s AI technology is deeply personalized. It doesn’t just aggregate reviews or ratings; it learns from individual user preferences and behaviors, offering tailor-made travel suggestions that evolve over time. Jaunt prides itself on offering a top notch user experience by designing to be more intuitive and engaging. This includes innovative features like the swiping functionality and chat-within-a-chat, which adds an element of interactivity and ease not found in other platforms.
