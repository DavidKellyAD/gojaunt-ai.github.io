Delete your account in the Jaunt app

To delete your jaunt account in the app, navigate to: Settings > Privacy > Account Deletion > Delete. When you confirm the deletion of your account you will be logged out.

How to delete your Jaunt account by email

To delete your account now please email deleteaccount@gojaunt.ai. Please include your phone number so we can verify your account and Jaunt customer service will delete your account as soon as possible.
